ClassicGlas FRP Fiberglass Columns by Melton Classics

The ClassicGlas™ FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Polymer) Columns by Melton Classics provide a perfect blend of timeless elegance, durability, and ease of installation, making them an ideal choice for both residential and commercial spaces seeking a long-lasting, low-maintenance column solution.

Exceptional Durability with Classic Design

Made from high-quality fiberglass, these columns are built to withstand extreme weather conditions, moisture, and temperature changes without cracking, splitting, or warping. Unlike traditional wood or stone columns, ClassicGlas columns require minimal upkeep and are designed to last. Melton Classics offers a variety of styles, such as Tuscan, Doric, Corinthian, and Composite, allowing for a unique architectural fit for any project.

Lightweight and Easy Installation

The lightweight structure of ClassicGlas FRP Columns simplifies installation and lowers costs. Despite being lightweight, they are structurally sound and capable of supporting substantial loads in various applications. They are available in various sizes and can be customized for specific project requirements.

Endless Customization Options

Melton Classics provides an extensive selection of shapes, sizes, and finishes to match any architectural design. ClassicGlas columns can be finished in nearly any color, making them an adaptable choice for both contemporary and traditional spaces. Coordinating FRP pilasters and bases are also available for seamless design integration.

Eco-Friendly and Cost-Effective

ClassicGlas FRP Columns are an environmentally friendly option. Made from sustainable materials, these columns are built for longevity, reducing replacement frequency. They offer a cost-effective alternative to traditional columns without sacrificing quality or durability.

Applications

Whether enhancing a historic structure or adding character to a new building, ClassicGlas FRP Columns are suitable for a variety of applications, including:

  • Porches
  • Patios
  • Porticos
  • Colonnades
  • Entranceways

With Melton Classics, you’re investing in columns that add elegance and value to any space while withstanding the test of time.
